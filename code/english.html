Today's Vocabulary

    Grammar

    Part 1: Nouns

    Nouns are words that name a person, place, thing, or idea. For example: "cat," "city," and "happiness."

    Part 2: Verbs

    Verbs are words that describe actions or states. For example: "run," "jump," and "is."

    Part 3: Adjectives

    Adjectives are words that describe nouns. For example: "happy," "blue," and "tall."
